our vision

Empower Students to Innovate

To build a strong research community that inspires creativity, teamwork, and innovation by providing students with the necessary resources, mentorship, and opportunities. We empower students to develop groundbreaking ideas, collaborate across disciplines, and transform their research into impactful real-world solutions.

We envision a dynamic research community where students are empowered to push the boundaries of knowledge, creativity, and innovation. By fostering a culture of collaboration, mentorship, and resource-sharing, we aim to equip aspiring researchers with the tools and opportunities needed to develop groundbreaking ideas. Our commitment is to transform academic curiosity into real-world impact, bridging disciplines and inspiring the next generation of innovators to drive meaningful change.

our mission

Inspiring Ideas, Creating Impact

The Student Research Council (SRC) supports and empowers students in their research journey by providing funding, mentorship, and opportunities to showcase their innovative ideas. We create a collaborative ecosystem where students can develop solutions for real-time problems, gain industry exposure, and protect their inventions.

Facilities

funding

Seed Funding

We provide financial assistance to students with groundbreaking ideas, ensuring that innovation is not hindered by a lack of resources. By supporting early-stage projects, we empower young researchers to develop transformative solutions, bridging the gap between ideation and execution to drive impactful advancements in technology and science.

guidance

Project Guidance

We identify and recognize outstanding student projects that demonstrate creativity, feasibility, and real-world impact. Through mentorship and exposure, we nurture talent and provide the right platforms for students to refine their ideas.

Upskill

Elevating Technical Minds

Through technical symposiums, hackathons, and innovation challenges, we foster an environment for knowledge exchange, skill development, and industry collaboration. These events provide students with opportunities to present their research, network with experts, and gain valuable insights, inspiring them to excel in their respective technical and scientific domains.

Invention protection

IPR Assistance

We educate students on Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), patents, and technology commercialization. By conducting workshops and knowledge-sharing sessions, we equip researchers with essential skills to protect their ideas, secure legal rights, and understand the commercialization landscape, ensuring that their innovations receive the recognition and protection they deserve.
