About

An intra college technical symposium organized by SRC in collaboration with department associations. In this cherished annual event more than 40 events are planned to be held with a overall prize pool of more than 1,00,000 Lakh Rupees. In a aim to motivate young engineering mind in the field of technical pursuit and challenging them in order to equip them better with up-to-date trends.


In this spectacular two-day event, the Student Research Council will host three dynamic inter-disciplinary events, while senior associations collectively organize an impressive 33 events with each association curating three exciting events. To inspire and engage first-year students, Ignite Association will spearhead five exclusive events.
